About Me

Hello! My name is Lauryn and I love books. I am currently a senior at UW-Madison majoring in political science and communications. Why didn't I major in English if I like books so much? Honestly, I don't know! My decision probably came from the idea that I should chose a major with which I can get a good job and whatever but I should have majored in English...and poli sci because I love that degree. I plan on getting my masters in Library and Information Science after taking a year or two off to save some money and read a lot of books. I'm excited to be a librarian though because I will be able to work with people and reccommend some great reads, which is also what I'm doing on this website! If I had an actual group of people that was interested in me, I imagine I would include a "frequently asked questions" section, but since I do not, I will anticipate your questions and include some (fake) FAQ's here!

FAQ's

What is your favorite book?

Great question! My favorite book is A Little Life by Hanya Yanagihara. I also really like The House on the Cerulean Sea by TJ Klune and Writers and Lovers by Lily King.

What is your least favorite book?

Also a great question! I really disliked The Hating Game by Sally Thorne and Death in Her Hands by Ottessa Moshfegh.

Why are you qualified to give book reviews?

I'm not really sure I am! But they're fun and I read more books than the average person so that's something.

Ideal reading scenario?

Comfy chair (preferrably pink or something fun), cat on lap, fireplace nearby, iced coffee on the table next to me, big chunky blanket over me and the kitty.
